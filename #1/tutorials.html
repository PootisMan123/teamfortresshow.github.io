The Basics

Welcome to the Tutorial!, before we start off, let's look through the basic mechanics and termonology used in the game so you dont get confused later on.

TF2 Terminology and Mechanics

Knockback
A force which moves the player in a direction because of one or multiple objects hitting the player in a specific direction.

Respawn
A mechanic where the player is placed back into the game after dying.

Taunting
The act of a player performing a (normally) harmless animated action used to taunt another player.

DeathCam
A picture is taken ingame of the place where you died or the person which killed you.

Criticals
A game mechanic in TF2 which triples the base damage dealt by the player for a short duration. They are also abbreviated to crits/crit.

Hitscan
A weapon mechanic where the damage dealt by a weapon is instantaneous from when you fire to where you are aiming at.

Projectile
A weapon mechanic in which a weapon would shoot out a projectile which will travel at a certain rate, before hitting a target and causing damage.

Hitbox
A game mechanic where the game places invisible boxes over your player model which when comes in contact with an enemy projectile or hitscan, would register damage on the player. Each class in the game has a diffrent hitbox.

Damage Ramp-Up
Terminology which describes the increase in damage of a weapon because of a variable

Damage Fall-off
Terminology which describes the decrease in damage of the weapon because of a variable, it is normally used to describe the decrease in damage of a weapon because of the further away you are from the target.

Headshot
Terminology which describes an increase in damage (normally a critical hit) because of shooting your weapon at an enemy's head.

Healthpool
Terminology used to describe a character's original health.
